Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay?

Payment is due on delivery. We accept Cash, bKash, Nagad, and Rocket at the PrintEase campus desk. Partial upfront payment may be required for bulk orders.

How long does it take?

Standard orders are typically ready within 1–2 hours during regular business hours. You can track the live status from your dashboard.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes! You can cancel any order directly from your dashboard as long as it hasn't entered the "Printing" phase.
